What is a drawing review?

A drawing review is a service that quickly pays for itself in reducing waste and time until the final prototype is ready.

During review the CAD documents are checked for a range potential problems. Broadly these fall into three seperate categories; geometric inaccuracies dimensional inaccuracies, CAM toolpathing.

A good example of a common problem are holecuts for bolts which are not dimensioned for a non-interference fit tolerance.
